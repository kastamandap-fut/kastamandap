About Us

WHERE FOOTBALL
MEETS NATURE

Kastamandap Futsal Academy is Kathmandu's best open-roof futsal court — and if you haven't played here yet, you're missing out. Located at Kumari Marg, we offer a stunning open-roof pitch surrounded by nature, where fresh mountain air and genuine football vibes come together like nowhere else in the city.

Open-Roof Design

Breathe fresh Kathmandu air while you play. Our open-roof court offers a unique outdoor futsal experience.

Peaceful Environment

Surrounded by greenery and nature — a calm, refreshing atmosphere you won't find anywhere else in the city.

👥

Community Football Vibes
